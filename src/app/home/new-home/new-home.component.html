ABOUT US

Ralba Technologies is committed to making Augmented Reality technology available to all. Its platform makes it possible for budget-conscious neighborhood stores of all sizes to maintain an aesthetically assertive global online presence that takes full-advantage of advanced Virtual Reality technology.

HOW IT WORKS

As a vendor, the platform makes it easy for you to create your own customized eyeglass store by uploading one or more images of the eyeglass models in your inventory. Or if you prefer, the platform allows you to act solely as an order fulfiller for any models that are part of your own inventory, and that are listed on any of its stores, without creating your own.
